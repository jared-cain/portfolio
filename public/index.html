Jared Cain

Full Stack Web Developer & Dev/Ops Engineer

Greetings! My name is Jared Cain and I am a Full Stack Web Developer and DevOps Engineer, with a focus on Javascript and Python/Django; although I enjoy challenges of many kinds! I am self starting and completely self taught; and have been developing/deploying in many languages as a hobby for the last 4+ years!. However, I learned Git over a year and a half ago and am still updating my Github!

Just a quick rundown, this website is bundled and built with Grunt, served with Node.js, and hosted on Heroku. Below in the Portfolio section, you'll notice I have a fascination with React/JSX. I plan on updating my Portfolio to run on React completely in the near future.

I can build and deploy apps with Grunt, Gulp, Webpack and know how to use them with Linux & Windows i.e using install hooks, ssh and filesystem etc. I know my way around HTML5/CSS3 & SVG. I am also comfortable with CDN's, SAAS, PAAS, and other cloud resources! As for data, I have working knowledge of MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQLite, MongoDB, Redis, and other enterprise types of data/app management like Firebase! On top of my technical abilities, I am also competent agile developer, XP (Extreme Programmer), TDDer & BDDer (in both Javascript and Python!) Feel free to email below, call, or otherwise get in touch using one of the social hooks so we can chat!
